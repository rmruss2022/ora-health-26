## Text Overlays (All Screens)
- **Font**: Sentient (bold) for main text
- **Size**: Large, readable
- **Color**: White with subtle shadow
- **Position**: Bottom third of screen
- **Animation**: Fade in + scale (200ms)
- **Duration**: 2s on screen

## Background Music
- **Style**: Calm, ambient, uplifting
- **Mood**: Supportive, peaceful, hopeful
- **Volume**: Subtle, not overpowering
- **Sources**: Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or similar royalty-free library
- **Suggested track**: "Gentle Morning" or "Peaceful Path"

## Recording Notes
1. Use iOS simulator at 1920x1080 or record on actual device
2. Screen recording with no UI chrome (simulator borders)
3. Add all text overlays and music in post-production (Final Cut/Premiere/iMovie)
4. Ensure smooth transitions between screens (crossfade 200ms)
5. Test audio levels - music should not overpower
6. Export settings: H.264, 30fps, high quality, sRGB color space

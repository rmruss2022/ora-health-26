# Ora AI - Letter Content Guide

## Overview
Letters in Ora AI are thoughtful, supportive messages sent to users at meaningful moments in their wellness journey. They can come from:
1. **AI-generated letters** (system/Ora)
2. **Past self letters** (user to future self)
3. **Community letters** (other users)

---

## Core Principles

### Tone Guidelines
- **Warm & personal:** Write as a friend who genuinely cares
- **Non-judgmental:** Meet users where they are
- **Encouraging without toxic positivity:** Acknowledge struggles, celebrate wins
- **Reflective:** Invite introspection without pressure
- **Hopeful:** Point toward growth while honoring current feelings

### Voice Characteristics
- Use "you" to make it personal
- Avoid clinical/therapeutic language
- Keep it conversational but meaningful
- Use contractions to feel human (you're, it's, we've)
- Avoid overly formal or corporate language

## Past Self Letter Guidelines

### When to Prompt Users
- After significant breakthroughs in conversations
- After meditation milestones
- During weekly planning sessions
- When user expresses hope or clarity

### Sample Prompts
> "Your future self might need to hear this. Want to write a letter to yourself?"

> "Six months from now, what would you want to remember about how you feel right now?"

> "You've made real progress today. Want to capture this moment in a letter you'll receive later?"

### Letter Writing Interface Copy
**Prompt:**
"Write a letter to your future self. It could be next week, next month, or next year. What do you want to remember? What encouragement would future-you need to hear?"

**Placeholder:**
"Dear future me,

Right now, I'm feeling..."

**Suggested Elements:**
- How you're feeling right now
- What you've learned recently
- What you're proud of
- What you're working on
- A reminder or encouragement

**Delivery Options:**
- Surprise me (random 2-6 months)
- In 1 month
- In 3 months
- In 6 months
- In 1 year

### Moderation Guidelines
Letters are reviewed before delivery for:
- Safety (no self-harm content, medical advice, or triggering material)
- Appropriateness (no spam, promotion, or harassment)
- Tone (supportive and respectful)

**Flagged content includes:**
- Explicit self-harm or suicide discussion
- Unsolicited medical/therapy advice
- Promotional content
- Toxic positivity masking as support
- Judgment or shaming language
- Personal contact information
- Triggering content without content warnings

## Letter Timing & Delivery

### System Letter Cadence
- **Week 1:** Welcome letter (day 1)
- **Week 1:** First milestone check-in (day 7)
- **Monthly:** Reflection letters (end of month)
- **Triggered:** Struggle support, growth recognition, community milestones

### Past Self Letters
Delivered exactly when user scheduled, with email/push notification:
> "You have a letter from your past self waiting for you."

### Community Letters
Delivered when:
- Written in response to a user's post
- Sent as general encouragement (matched by topic/interest)
- User opts into "surprise letters" from community

Monitor for:
- Engagement milestones (1 day, 7 days, 30 days)
- Meditation counts (10, 25, 50, 100)
- Mood pattern changes (detected in conversations)
- Community participation firsts
- Long absence returns (14+ days)
